Thebargain

A webpage-based micro roleplaying game about making deals with the devil.

Start

You will need 3+ people and some kind of timer.

Choose one person to play the Devil. All the other players are Summoners. The Summoners are summoning the Devil to make a bargain. They have 7 minutes together to make all of their deals. The Devil also controls the negotiations: they may point to a Summoner, and the others must fall silent.

You will have exactly 7 minutes to negotiate. When the time is up, read the epilogue section at the bottom.

Summoners, read the section below. Devil, skip to the Devil heading.

Summoners

As a summoner, you are here to make a deal with the Devil. Of course, the Devil will need something in return. Your soul, your firstborn, eternal servitude, that sort of thing.

When everyone is ready to begin, click your link below, and start the timer.

Epilogue

After 7 minutes, the deals are sealed. Both parties reveal their points.
If the Devil got more points out of the deal, the Devil describes the "fine print" of the deal, and the unexpected.
If the summoner got more points, the summoner has tricked the.
If the points are equal, it is a fair deal.
If one of the summoners failed to make a deal, the Devil dispatches a demon to kill the summoner. Of course, this is quite annoying for all sides.
