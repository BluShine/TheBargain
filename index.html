Thebargain

A webpage-based micro roleplaying game about making deals with the devil.

Start

You will need 3+ people and some kind of timer.

Choose one person to play the Devil. All the other players are Summoners. The Summoners are summoning the Devil to make a bargain. They have 7 minutes together to make all of their deals.

Summoners, read the section below. Devil, skip to the Devil heading.
