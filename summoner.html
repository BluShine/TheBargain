The Summoner's Desires

These are the things that you want as a Summoner. You don't need to get all of them, but you should try to get a good deal. Even if you can get everything, if the Devil is ripping you off, it won't end well for you.

Of course, the Devil won't give you what you want out of the kindness of his heart. You have to give them something in return. Here's what you can offer:

  1. Your soul
  2. Your life
  3. Your eternal servitude
  4. Your firstborn child
